Agentic AI for Smart Agriculture Advisory

An intelligent advisory platform that analyzes soil data, weather data, and crop history to provide farmers with smart crop suggestions, irrigation advice, disease alerts, and weather-based recommendations.

Explore Architecture See Features

Problem Statement

Why this project matters

Many farmers still depend on guesswork or traditional decision-making, which becomes unreliable under changing environmental conditions. Our system addresses this gap using intelligent analysis and personalized advisory support.

Current Challenge

Lack of accurate and real-time agricultural guidance leads to poor farming decisions, reduced productivity, and increased risk in crop management.

Major Outcomes of the Problem

Wrong crop selection, crop damage due to unpredictable weather, overuse of water and fertilizers, delayed disease detection, and financial loss for farmers.

Our Solution

A smart advisory platform powered by machine learning and agentic AI that turns raw field data into useful, actionable recommendations.

System Design

Project architecture

The system takes three primary inputs—soil data, weather data, and crop dataset— processes them through data analysis, a Random Forest model, and an Agentic AI decision engine, then generates practical recommendations for farmers.

01

Input Layer

Soil data includes pH, moisture, and nutrients; weather data includes temperature, rainfall, and humidity; crop dataset includes crop information and history.

02

Processing Layer

Data is preprocessed and analyzed before passing through a Random Forest machine learning model and an Agentic AI decision engine.

03

Output Layer

The platform generates crop suggestions, irrigation advice, disease alerts, and weather advice as smart advisory support for farmers.
